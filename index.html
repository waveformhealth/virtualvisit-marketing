The features

Check out what Virtual Visit brings!

Easy setup

No account or login for the patient is needed, simply start a Virtual Visit and invite someone to the room!

Invite through text

Patient just has to enter a phone number and their friend or family member will receive a text with a link to join the Visit.

Video chat

A patient can have a meaningful Virtual Visit with a private video chat, keeping everyone involved safe!

Minimal

Virtual Visit was designed for ease of use, requiring only a few buttons to be pressed to start a Visit!

Open source

Our code is open source, and will stay that way, so you know exatcly whats going on behind the scenes!

Device agnostic

Mobile phone or computer, anyone can join a Virtual Visit on any device that can run a browser!
